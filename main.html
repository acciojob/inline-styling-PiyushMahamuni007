Flowers photos

Flowers are the reproductive structures of angiosperms, evolving ~150 million years ago. They facilitate seed production through pollination (via insects, birds, wind) and serve as vital ecosystem food sources. A typical flower includes petals, sepals, male stamens (producing pollen), and female pistils (developing seeds), ranging in size and color.

Roses pictures

Roses are perennial flowering shrubs of the genus Rosa in the family Rosaceae, featuring over 100 species primarily native to Asia, with many cultivated for their beauty and fragrance.
